Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if Astral Draw is legal in my country?

You should consult with a legal professional in your jurisdiction or check with your local gaming commission. Laws vary significantly between countries and even within regions of the same country. We cannot provide legal advice regarding your specific situation.

What happens if I participate from a restricted jurisdiction?

If you participate from a restricted jurisdiction, you may be violating local laws, which could result in legal consequences. Additionally, we may be required to void any winnings and restrict account access if we determine participation violates our terms of service or applicable laws.

Are my winnings taxable?

Tax obligations vary by jurisdiction. In many countries, gambling winnings are subject to taxation. You are responsible for understanding and complying with tax laws in your country of residence. We recommend consulting with a tax professional regarding your specific situation.

How does Astral Draw ensure fair play?

We use Hedera Hashgraph's consensus algorithm to generate provably fair random numbers. Each draw's outcome is recorded on the immutable distributed ledger, allowing complete transparency and verification of results. Our smart contracts are publicly auditable.

What identification do I need to provide?

We may require identity verification for certain transactions or withdrawals to comply with anti-money laundering regulations. This typically includes government-issued ID and proof of address. Specific requirements depend on your jurisdiction and transaction amounts.

Can I set deposit limits on my account?

Yes, our platform includes responsible gaming tools that allow you to set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits. You can also implement cooling-off periods or self-exclusion if needed. These features are accessible from your account settings.

How are prizes distributed to winners?

Prizes are automatically distributed to winners' Hedera wallets immediately after each draw conclusion. The process is transparent and recorded on the blockchain. Winners receive 70% of the prize pool, with the remainder allocated to platform operations and future prizes.
